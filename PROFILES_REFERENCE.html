Silvia Lever — Profile Reference Sheet

Generated from firmware segment tables · values mirror silvia_lever_main.ino / config.h. Use this to verify a brew's pressure curve against the design. Last updated 2026-05-28.

How to read these. The blue line is the commanded pressure setpoint over time — what the firmware aims for; the PI(D) loop drives the pump to track it. Preinfuse length is variable (it ends on first drips or a time cap), so it's drawn at a representative length and annotated. Pressure-gated and time-gated phase exits are marked. The setpoint slews continuously between phases at the listed ramp rate (bar/sec); it does not jump.
━ pressure setpoint (bar) FILL/RAMP setpoint rising BLOOM/PREINFUSE low-pressure soak HOLD steady DECLINE taper down ↓ = ends on first drips / weight · ⏱ = time cap · ⤒ = pressure target

Setpoint = what the firmware commands; actual puck pressure tracks it via the PI(D) pump loop (may lag/overshoot slightly on very fine grinds). Phase exits fire on the first criterion met. "Pot takeover": rotate the pump knob >10 % mid-shot and control hands to you bumplessly. Full engineering detail in PROFILES.md; quick picker in PROFILES_CHEATSHEET.md.
